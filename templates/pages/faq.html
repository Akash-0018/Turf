Frequently Asked Questions

Making a booking is easy! Simply select your preferred date and time slot from our booking page, choose your sport type, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the booking time are eligible for a full refund. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged the full booking amount.

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and net banking. All transactions are secure and processed through our payment gateway.

Our facilities include football turfs, cricket nets, basketball courts, and badminton courts. Each facility is maintained to professional standards and includes basic amenities.

Basic equipment is available for rent at nominal charges. However, we recommend bringing your own equipment for the best experience.

Our facilities are open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week. However, some facilities might have different timings based on maintenance schedules.
